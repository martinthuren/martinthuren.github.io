Privacy Policy for Fragrance Finder

Last updated: January 22, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Fragrance Finder ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects the following information:

  • Fragrance preferences and browsing history on Fragrantica.com
  • Extension usage data for improving our service

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide fragrance recommendations
  • Improve our extension's functionality
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

Third-Party Access

We do not share your data with any third parties. The extension only interacts with Fragrantica.com to gather publicly available fragrance information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored by the extension
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Opt-out of data collection by disabling the extension

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at fragrancefinder@mail.com.
